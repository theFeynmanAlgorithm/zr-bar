Guest Favorites

Espresso Martini

  • 1 shot of espresso
  • 1 oz vodka
  • 1/2 oz coffee liqueur
  • 1/2 oz simple syrup
  • 1/4 oz cream
Directions: Shake ingredients, strain into up glass. Garnish with a coffee bean.

Mojito

  • ~8 mint leaves
  • 2 oz unaged rum
  • 1/2 oz lime juice
  • 1/2 oz simple syrup
  • club soda
Directions: Muddle mint in highball with all ingredients but soda. Top with soda. Garnish with mint and a lime wheel.

Moscow Mule

  • 2 oz vodka
  • 1/2 oz lime juice
  • 1/4 oz simple syrup
  • ~4 oz ginger beer
Directions: Stir all but ginger beer in highball with ice. Add ginger beer. Garnish with a lime wedge.

Tequila Sunrise

  • 1/2 oz grenadine
  • 1 1/2 oz blanco tequila
  • 3/4 oz triple sec
  • 1/2 oz lime juice
  • 2 oz orange juice
Directions: Pour grenadine into highball before ice. Shake remaining ingredients and layer onto grenadine.

Classics

Daiquiri

  • 2 oz unaged rum
  • 1 oz lime juice
  • 1/2 oz simple syrup
Directions: Shake ingredients, strain into up glass. Garnish with a lime wheel.

Gin and Tonic

  • 2 oz gin
  • ~6 oz tonic
  • Juice of 1 lime wedge
Directions: Squeeze and drop lime into highball filled with ice. Pour in gin and tonic at same time.

Margarita

  • 2 oz blanco tequila
  • 1 oz lime juice
  • 1 oz triple sec
  • 1/4 oz simple syrup
Directions: Shake ingredients, strain into salted up glass. Garnish with a lime wheel.

Negroni

  • 1 1/2 oz gin
  • 3/4 oz Campari
  • 3/4 oz rosso vermouth
Directions: Shake ingredients, serve in rocks glass over ice. Garnish with a lemon wheel.

Old Fashioned

  • 2 oz bourbon/rye
  • 1/3 oz simple syrup
  • 2 dashes Angostura bitters
Directions: Stir ingredients, serve in rocks glass over ice. Garnish with a lemon peel.

Whiskey Sour

  • 2 oz bourbon
  • 1 oz lemon juice
  • 1/2 oz simple syrup
  • 2 dashes Angostura bitters
Directions: Shake ingredients, serve in rocks glass over ice. Garnish with a lemon wheel.
