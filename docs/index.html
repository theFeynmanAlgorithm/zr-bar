Guest Favorites

Espresso Martini

  • 1 shot of espresso
  • 1 oz vodka
  • 1/2 oz coffee liqueur
  • 1/2 oz simple syrup
  • 1/4 oz cream
Directions: Shake ingredients, strain into up glass. Garnish with a coffee bean.

Mojito

  • ~8 mint leaves
  • 2 oz unaged rum
  • 1/2 oz lime juice
  • 1/2 oz simple syrup
  • club soda
Directions: Muddle mint in highball with all ingredients but soda. Top with soda. Garnish with mint and a lime wheel.

Moscow Mule

  • 2 oz vodka
  • 1/2 oz lime juice
  • 1/4 oz simple syrup
  • ~4 oz ginger beer
Directions: Stir all but ginger beer in highball with ice. Add ginger beer. Garnish with a lime wedge.

Tequila Sunrise

  • 1/2 oz grenadine
  • 1 1/2 oz blanco tequila
  • 3/4 oz triple sec
  • 1/2 oz lime juice
  • 2 oz orange juice
Directions: Pour grenadine into highball before ice. Shake remaining ingredients and layer onto grenadine.

Gin and Tonic

  • 2 oz gin
  • ~6 oz tonic
  • Juice of 1 lime wedge
Directions: Squeeze and drop lime into highball filled with ice. Pour in gin and tonic at same time.

Long Island

  • ~2 oz Coke
  • 3/4 oz vodka
  • 3/4 oz gin
  • 3/4 oz tequila
  • 3/4 oz rum
  • 3/4 oz triple sec
  • 3/4 oz lemon juice
Directions: Add coke to a highball glass, stir remaining ingredients and pour over top. Garnish with a lemon wedge.

Margarita

Directions: Shake ingredients, strain into salted up glass. Garnish with a lime wheel.

High Five

  • 1/2 oz Campari
  • 1 oz grapefruit juice
  • 1/2 oz lime juice
  • 3/4 oz simple syrup
Directions: Shake ingredients, double strain into a coupe. Garnish with a high five.

Hot Toddy

  • 1.5 oz rye whiskey
  • 1/4 oz lemon juice
  • 1/2 oz simple syrup
  • 1 dash angostura bitters
  • ~4-6 oz boiling water
Directions: Stir ingredients, add boiling water. Garnish with nutmeg and a lemon wedge.
